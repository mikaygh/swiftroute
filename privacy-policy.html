Privacy Statement

Swift Route Ltd. is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) of Ghana. We value your trust and are dedicated to handling your information lawfully, fairly, and securely.

Our Commitment to You

  • Lawful Processing: We collect and process personal data only for legitimate business purposes and in line with the principles of the Data Protection Act.
  • Transparency: We ensure you are informed about the type of personal data we collect, the purpose of processing, and your rights.
  • Confidentiality & Security: We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the personal data that is relevant and necessary for the services we provide.
  • Retention: We keep your data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

Collection of Personal Data

  1. Automatic Information Collection: We may automatically collect information when you interact with our platforms (e.g., device details, logs). We also use cookies to customize your experience and improve security.
  2. Information from Downloads: When you download or use our digital platforms, we may receive location and device information (including a unique identifier) to provide location-based services.
  3. Physically or Digitally Provided Information: We collect data you provide when you create or update an account, complete forms/surveys, or submit documents (e.g., IDs, correspondence, agreements).
  4. Third Parties: We may receive information from relatives, guardians, financial institutions, vendors, and service providers.
  5. Social Media: We may collect information from engagements with us on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and WhatsApp.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To communicate with you regarding services, updates, or support
  • To protect the security of our systems and users

Your Rights Under the Act

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual requirements
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent, where processing is based on consent

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that collect information about your actions on our site. We use cookies to recognize users, customize content and services, measure promotions, enhance security, mitigate risk, prevent fraud, and improve trust and safety. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser. Our cookie use applies to our websites and platforms only.
